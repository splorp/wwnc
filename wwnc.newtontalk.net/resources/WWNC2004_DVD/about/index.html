The inaugural Worldwide Newton Conference took place on September 4th and 5th, 2004. Dozens of Newton users and aficionados from around the globe gathered in Paris, France for lectures, presentations, workshops, discussions, and camaraderie. The event was presented by the Worldwide Newton Association under the direction of Paul Guyot.
